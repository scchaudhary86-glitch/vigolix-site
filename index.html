About Vigolix

Canadian Trade Supplier

VIGOLIX is a Calgary-based importer and trade supplier of PEX plumbing products, serving distributors, contractors, and wholesalers across Canada.

We supply selected products to meet Canadian market requirements and focus on long-term trade relationships.

Importer Model

VIGOLIX imports finished PEX plumbing products and supplies them to the Canadian trade market. We do not manufacture.

Canadian Supply Focus

All supply and fulfillment is structured for Canadian distribution channels and trade accounts.

Trade Customers Only

We supply distributors, wholesalers, and contractors. Not for direct consumer sale.
