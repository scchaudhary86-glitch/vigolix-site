01

IAPMO cUPC Certified — File #10405

All Vigolix PEX fittings are manufactured and listed under IAPMO File #10405, carrying cUPC certification recognized by building inspectors across all Canadian provinces.

ASTM F1960-2024 & F2159

Products conform to the latest ASTM standards for cold expansion and insert/crimp PEX fittings — the same standards referenced in the National Plumbing Code of Canada.
